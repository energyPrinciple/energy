Half Day Program - How Energy Management affects performance

Our half day program introduces participants to the definition of energy and how energy management impacts performance.

Using the principles of high performance research and concentrating on the dimensions of physical and emotional energy we teach participants how to keep themselves in high performance mode for longer periods of time by systematically implementing simple behaviours to renewing their energy throughout the day.

Participants will leave the session with an identified plan of the most important new behaviours to implement in order to deliver their best and remain resilient in the face of relentless demand.
